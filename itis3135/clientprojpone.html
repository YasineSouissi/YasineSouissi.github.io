Project overview

  • Describe your application and its purpose: My application is a professional portfolio for a close friend of mine, he is a film major at UNCW and wants something that he can send to prospective employers.
  • Intended users of the website: Mainly our focus will be on presenting his data in a way that will look professional for the employers who will be the audience looking at it.
  • Overview of the content of the website: We are going to have all of his about information as a tab in the nav bar as well as any events hes worked to coordinate, a composite of all of his videos, a page with inspirations and works he draws from as well as any projects that he may not have filmed or directed but played a large role in edition.
